# Data, Information, Knowledge, and Wisdom (DIKW Model)

## Data
Data refers to raw facts and figures without any context or meaning. It is unprocessed and unorganized.

Examples:
- Numbers: 25, 100, 3.14
- Symbols: A, B, #
- Raw values: 36, 39, 41

Data alone does not provide understanding.
